Our Medical Services

Ensuring your health and safety during your stay

24/7 On-call Doctor

Our hotel offers a 24/7 on-call doctor service to attend to any medical emergencies or concerns you may have during your stay. Our qualified doctors are just a phone call away.

First Aid and Emergency Care

We provide first aid and emergency care services for minor injuries and health issues. Our staff is trained in basic first aid to assist you promptly.

Pharmacy Services

For your convenience, we have an in-house pharmacy where you can get essential medications and health supplies. Our staff can also assist in getting prescriptions filled.

Wellness and Health Programs

We offer various wellness programs, including health check-ups, fitness classes, and nutritional counseling to ensure you maintain a healthy lifestyle during your stay.

Special Medical Requests

If you have any special medical needs or require specific medical equipment, please inform us in advance, and we will do our best to accommodate your requirements.
