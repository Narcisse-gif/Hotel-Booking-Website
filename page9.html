Cultural Activities at the Hotel

Discover the unique cultural events organized by our hotel

Local Art Exhibition

Come and admire the works of local artists in our art gallery, open daily from 10 AM to 6 PM.

Traditional Cooking Workshop

Join our cooking workshop and learn to prepare traditional dishes with our chef. Workshops are held every Wednesday and Saturday at 3 PM.

Live Music Night

Join us for a night of live music every Friday starting at 8 PM. Enjoy performances by local musicians in a friendly atmosphere.

Historical Guided Tour

Discover the history of our region with our historical guided tour. Tours are held every Monday and Thursday at 10 AM.
